FAQ

Find answers to common questions about anxiety management and our services.

How can i manage anxiety?

Managing anxiety can be achieved through various techniques such as meditation, deep breathing exercises, and listening to calming music. It's important to find what works best for you and incorporate it into your daily routine.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services to help individuals manage their anxiety, including meditation sessions, breath techniques, a diary for noting thoughts and feelings, and a collection of random calming pictures.

Is FeelSafe free to use?

Yes, FeelSafe is completely free to use. We believe that everyone should have access to tools and resources for managing anxiety without any financial barriers.

How can I contact you?

If you have any further questions or need assistance, please feel free to reach out to us. You can contact us through the provided form or send us an email at support@feelsafe.com

Can I trust FeelSafe?

FeelSafe takes your privacy and security seriously. We have implemented strict measures to ensure the safety of your personal information and provide a secure platform for anxiety management.
