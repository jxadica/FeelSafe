Equal breathing

Step into the world of Resonant or Coherent Breathing, a technique that invites you to synchronize your breath with the natural rhythms of your body. Also known as paced or coherent breathing, this practice involves maintaining a specific breath rate to induce a state of balance and harmony. By aligning your breath with a resonant frequency, you can create a physiological and psychological coherence that promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and enhances overall well-being. Join us on this journey of intentional breathing to discover the resonance within.

Steps for Resonant or Coherent Breathing:

Step 1:Find a Comfortable Seat:
Sit in a comfortable position, either on the floor with crossed legs or in a chair with your feet flat on the ground. Ensure your spine is straight, and your shoulders are relaxed.
Step 2:Relax and Center:
Close your eyes if you feel comfortable doing so. Take a few natural breaths to settle into the present moment, letting go of any tension in your body.
Step 3:Choose a Breath Rate:
Select a specific breath rate to maintain throughout the practice. A common starting point is to inhale and exhale for a count of six seconds each. Adjust the count based on your comfort, but keep the inhalation and exhalation equal.
Step 4:Begin Inhaling:
Inhale slowly and steadily through your nose for the chosen count. Focus on a smooth and controlled breath, allowing your lungs to fill comfortably.
Step 5:Exhale with the Same Count:
Exhale smoothly and completely through your nose, matching the count used for the inhalation. Maintain a consistent pace and rhythm.
Step 6:Continue the Breath Cycle:
Repeat the inhalation and exhalation at the chosen breath rate. Keep the breath continuous and even, creating a seamless flow.
Step 7: Focus on Heart-Centered Breathing:
Shift your awareness to the area around your heart. Imagine that your breath is flowing in and out through the center of your chest, fostering a heart-centered experience.
