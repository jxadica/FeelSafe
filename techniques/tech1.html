Pursued Lip Breathing

Pursed lip breathing is a simple breathing technique that involves inhaling through the nose and exhaling through pursed lips. It is often recommended for individuals with respiratory conditions to improve their breathing efficiency.

Steps to Perform Pursed Lip Breathing:

Step 1:Sit in a Comfortable Position:
Start by finding a comfortable and relaxed sitting position. Sit up straight with your shoulders relaxed.
Step 2:Inhale Through the Nose:
Inhale slowly and gently through your nose. Focus on taking a deep breath to fill your lungs with air.
Step 3:Purse Your Lips:
Pucker or purse your lips, as if you were about to whistle. Keep your mouth closed, except for a small opening between your pursed lips.
Step 4:Exhale Slowly:
Exhale slowly and evenly through the small opening in your pursed lips. Make the exhalation longer than the inhalation, allowing more time for the exchange of gases in the lungs.
Step 5:Repeat:
Repeat the process for several breaths. Focus on making each breath controlled and relaxed.
